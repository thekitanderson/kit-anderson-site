Author · Artist · Amateur Stuntman

Kit Anderson is a North American author, artist, and athlete who operates a private media kitchen in Truckee, CA. He creates outsider fiction, fine art, and satirical ephemera that is never funny.

His work has won every prize and all of the accolades, but that's all bullshit. The only opinion that matters is your own.

Visual Practice

Art

Kit Anderson is a contemporary multi-media artist whose work exploits the natural surroundings of the Sierra Nevada as both subject and source of material. His practice spans fine art, sculpture, and satirical ephemera — all of it united by a single question: how do you express awe?

His work has found its way into important collections through exhibitions, art fairs, and alternative distribution. He completed a formal sculpture apprenticeship under Tedesco in 2014.

"A contemporary artist who exploits his natural surroundings as the well-spring for his work and source of materials — exploring themes of awe and wonder through the prism of fantasy, observation, and memory."
